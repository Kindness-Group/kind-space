Jerry Sanchez

Technology - Proficiency

  • Familiar with the UI of Google on PC and phone. He has been dedicated to Android devices after he was gifted an Android tablet.
  • Jerry uses the Google Chrome Web Browser as his default on all his devices and has been purchasing Chromebooks because of familiarity of the UI in comparison to Google Chrome's
  • Jerry has gained a liking to photography and looks for photo ops with people when there are kind actions taking place. He uses the Google technology built into his phone to enhance the photos he takes
  • Has a strong social media presence on Facebook and Instagram. Can navigate these sites, post updates, share photos, and engage with others' content. He is a member of a photography enthusiasts group where he spreads kindness through the sharing of his photos.
  • Is early on in his photography hobby and looks at tutorials online for help in creating high quality photos. He is taking online courses to learn more about the digital media stored on digital devices and how to enhance the media using software or hardware
  • Capable of resolving common technical issues, such as resetting passwords, troubleshooting internet connectivity problems, and updating software.

Loves and Hates

  • Loves movies that maintains focus on installing good values and care for others as the main motif. He is a fan of media that improves mood.
  • Dislikes media that do not focus on the positive and use negative undertones to portray the overall motif. Has no tolerance for negative and depressing music.
  • Enjoys working in his garden. His backyard is a sanctuary of blooming flowers, fresh herbs, and vegetables. He often shares his homegrown produce with neighbors.
  • Enjoys giving back to the community. Whether it's serving meals at the local shelter or organizing charity events, he's always looking for ways to make a positive impact.
  • Dislikes news media that panders to the bias of people who believe there is only one way to live life right. He believes that there are good and bad on both sides of the political spectrum.
  • Is an avid reader who enjoys everything from classic literature to contemporary novels. His home is filled with bookshelves that hold stories that are waiting to be explored.

Attitudes and Behaviors

  • A strong desire to nurture young minds through mentoring. He enjoys spending his free time mentoring students who need extra support, both academically and personally.
  • Despite life's challenges, Jerry maintains an optimistic outlook and believes in the goodness of people. Jerry's mind is always looking to the positive.
  • Is the sort of person who remembers birthdays, and lends a helping hand without being asked. Makes sure to make people feel seen and valued.
  • Over the years, he has helped numerous individuals find stability and hope. Some of his former mentees have gone on to achieve great success.
  • Has a natural ability to understand and share the feelings of others. This empathy drives him to help those who are struggling, as he genuinely cares about their well-being.
  • As a teacher and mentor, Jerry understands the importance of setting a positive example for his students and others. His volunteering efforts demonstrate the value of kindness and generosity.

User Story

  • As a user, I want to see various good deeds and actions posted on the site with location and various ad postings of human volunteerism that can be offered to and claimed by other users. Seeing all the positivity will help brighten my day.

Use Case

Jerry has been wanting to find other kind people and volunteer to join them in carrying out their good deeds. Jerry's friend Bob told him about a new site where there are people posting random acts of kindness on a regular basis. Jerry wants to see the site and read all the good deeds and see if the site is an excellent networking tool.

  • Pre-condition: Jerry has accessed the landing page for the website and is looking at the kind feed.
  • Post-condition: Jerry has viewed kind spots on the map and hearted numerous posts depicting kind deeds. He has created an account to become a contributing member of the site.

Interaction-Flow/User-Flow

  • Jerry views the most recent posts of kind and caring actions on the kind feed.
  • As he scrolls down the feed, he reacts to the posts with a heart.
  • He clicks the heart reaction on the bottom left of the post to have it show up as a reaction.
  • He reads the comments left by other kind doers on every post.
  • Jerry clicks on the comments section of the post to expand the comments section and reads comments.
  • The location where the kindness action took place is shown on the top portion of the post.
  • The location is clicked and Jerry is taken to a Map of the specific New Mexico region and sees the location pinned on the map.
  • Jerry sees other pins around and within the specific area and hovers his mouse pointer over it.
  • A pop-out appears with the name of the post and the name of the creator, along with the date.
  • Jerry clicks on the post title and is taken to the post itself, where he reads it with love and enthusiasm in mind.
  • Jerry clicks on the 'kindness ad space' where he sees many postings of people offering free clothing and food, along with peoples' time to teach and mentor.
  • Jerry likes what he sees and wants to contribute to the site by posting kindness, offering his time for helping others, and commenting on the kindness that others display.
  • Jerry signs up for an account and is taken to his account page.
