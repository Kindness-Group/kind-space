User Story

As a community-minded individual, I want to easily document and map acts of kindness I witness during my workday, so I can inspire others and visualize the spread of kindness in my neighborhood.

Use Case: Documenting a Chain of Kindness

Maria witnesses a customer paying for a homeless person's meal at her coffee shop, which leads to that person helping someone else later.

Pre-conditions

  • Maria is logged into the app
  • Location services are enabled
  • She's on her lunch break

Post-conditions

  • Two connected acts of kindness are posted
  • Both locations are marked on the kindness map
  • Daily kindness counter is updated

Interaction Flow

  1. Maria opens the app and taps the "Share Kindness" button
  2. App presents options for type of kindness post (Witnessed/Experienced/Connected Story)
  3. Maria selects "Connected Story" option
  4. App displays map for location tagging
  5. Maria confirms current location (coffee shop) for first act
  6. App prompts for story details
  7. Maria enters description of the first act (customer buying meal)
  8. Maria adds relevant tags (#PayItForward #CommunityKindness)
  9. App prompts for second location
  10. Maria drops pin at nearby park for second act
  11. Maria enters details of the connected act of kindness
  12. App shows preview of both stories and map markers
  13. Maria reviews and taps "Share Kindness"
  14. App confirms posting and adds two heart markers to the map
  15. App shows success message with total kindness acts in the area
  16. Maria's post appears in the main feed and on the map view
